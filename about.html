About

The volunteer database serves as a means to organize and gather information about volunteers and the locations that they are volunteering at. The volunteers personal information will be stored within the database, as well as information about locations, events, and event schedules. A user will be able to query the database to gather information such as where a voluteer is scheduled to be, how many hours they have been ther, where an event is located, the type of event, and other information.
